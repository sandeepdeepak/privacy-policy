Privacy Policy for Bills Upload Extension

Effective Date: [14 Oct 2024]

1. Introduction

The Bills Upload Extension ("we," "us," or "our") is a Chrome extension designed to help users upload commute bills from services like Ola, Uber, and Rapido. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our extension.

2. Information We Collect

  • Cookies: We may access cookies from your browser to authenticate users when uploading files.
  • Files: We collect the files you choose to upload for processing. No additional personal information is collected without your consent.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate file uploads.
  • To authenticate user sessions using cookies.
  • To improve our extension's functionality and user experience.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We may share information with third parties only if required by law or to protect our rights.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
